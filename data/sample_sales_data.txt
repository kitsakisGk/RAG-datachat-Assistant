Sales Data Analysis Report - Q4 2025

Executive Summary:
Our sales team achieved record-breaking results in Q4 2025, with total revenue reaching $2.5 million,
representing a 35% increase compared to Q3 2025. The primary drivers were strong performance in the
Enterprise segment and successful product launches.

- North America: $1.2M (48% of total revenue)
- Europe: $800K (32% of total revenue)
- Asia-Pacific: $500K (20% of total revenue)

Product Categories:
1. Software Licenses: $1.5M (60% of revenue)
2. Professional Services: $600K (24% of revenue)
3. Support Contracts: $400K (16% of revenue)

- Security Suite: $370K in sales

Customer Segments:

- Mid-Market (100-1000 employees): 25% of revenue
- SMB (<100 employees): 10% of revenue

Key Metrics:
- Average Deal Size: $45,000
- Sales Cycle: 42 days average
- Customer Acquisition Cost: $8,500
- Customer Lifetime Value: $125,000
- Win Rate: 32%

Top Sales Representatives:
1. Sarah Johnson - $380K in closed deals

Challenges and Opportunities:
The main challenge was longer sales cycles in the Enterprise segment due to budget approval processes.

Forecast for Q1 2026:

representing continued growth momentum.
